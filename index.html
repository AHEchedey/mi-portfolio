OVA is an investment fund built around a central promise: to invest disruptively in the most valuable, promising, and resilient assets.
By focusing on companies transforming the digital landscape, OVA identifies solutions capable of addressing contemporary challenges and generating significant and sustainable returns.

Technology

OVA recognizes the exceptional potential of the technology sector, where each breakthrough opens up promising investment opportunities.
With expertise in multi-residential, industrial, and commercial properties, OVA capitalizes on stable assets while anticipating growth opportunities through innovative and efficient optimization strategies aligned with current trends.

Real Estate

OVA values the inherent resilience of real estate, a sector capable of thriving despite economic fluctuations.
By supporting companies at the forefront of innovative treatments and connected devices, OVA maximizes long-term global health impact while ensuring solid and sustainable returns.

Health and Biotechnology

Rapid innovation in the healthcare sector is redefining the boundaries of care. OVA identifies strategic investment opportunities in groundbreaking therapeutic solutions and cutting-edge medical technologies.
Through international investments, particularly in tourism residences, OVA leverages the positive momentum of a rapidly growing sector, bolstered by a steady recovery following recent challenges.

Tourism and Hospitality

OVA believes in the power of high-quality destinations and personalized services to captivate travelers. We closely monitor consumption trends to seize the best market opportunities.
By staying attuned to emerging trends and evolving consumer needs, OVA identifies strategic opportunities that ensure sustainable returns.

Finance and Fintech

OVA values financial innovations that transform the customer experience. With a proactive approach, we position ourselves as a key player in this evolving and essential sector of the global economy.
By leveraging data analysis and campaign optimization, OVA identifies opportunities to capture consumer attention where it matters most.

Advertising and Digital Media

This strategic approach not only ensures better investment performance but also builds lasting relationships between brands and their audiences.
